FAQs

How to apply the masks on my PS4?

  • Download the mask you would like to apply from masks section.
  • Open Iconit v4.91 or later.

    • Create a set of icons (GROUPS)
    • Select 'Icons' mode on the main window
    • Select 'Menu' (Top-left-hand-corner)
    • Select 'Set Icon Group'
    • Click 'SET/UPDATE DEFAULT GROUP' NOTE: Make sure all icons are normal without any edits nor masks

    NOTE: All icons on the PS4 will be in the 'default group'. To make a custom group:

    • Click 'SET CUSTOM GROUP'
    • Pick the games for this group, type in the title for the group
    • Click 'SAVE'
    Apply Mask
    • Click 'Mask Maker' on Icons mode
    • Click 'Mask...' and choose the mask '.Zip'
    • Click 'Group...' and choose the group to apply the mask
    • Click 'BAKE ICONS' to apply the mask on all icons of the selected group
    • Click 'UPLOAD BAKED ICONS' to send the icons to the PS4

    What is the difference between icons masks? whats better?

    Icons are ready-to-use images with fixed frame designs. You cannot change their frame design ("Mask").

    Masks are customizable images where you can choose the image and frame you desire. You have two options:

    • Create a custom mask by yourself and apply it.
    • Choose a pre-made mask from the masks list.
