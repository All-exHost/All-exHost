FAQs

How to apply the masks on my PS4?

* Download the mask you would like to apply by simply clicking on the mask at the masks section.
* Open Iconit v4.91 or later, navigate to Iconit masks
* Select the downloaded mask
* Apply changes

How can I share my custom-made masks?

If you know how to design and willing to contribute your work, Kindly contact @Officialahmed0

What is the difference between icons & masks? whats better?

Icons are set and ready for use you cannot change anything to the icon, while masks are customizable images, you pick the image and the frame design. You can make one on your own and apply it or choose a pre-made mask .
